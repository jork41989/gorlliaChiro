History

Established in 1999.

Dr. Marshall Jacobs grew up in Rockaway, New Jersey. He is no stranger to aches and pains. Quite some years ago, he injured himself in a weight lifting accident. After great relief at the hands of a chiropractor, he decided to pursue the field as a career. Dr. Jacobs is not anti-medicine or any other form of healthcare that is effective and encourages healing. He discourages over-medication and he and his family live and promote a wellness lifestyle.He completed his undergraduate required studies at the University of Nebraska-Omaha before earning his Bachelor of Science degree in Human Biology and Doctor of Chiropractic degree in St. Louis, MO. The many post-doctoral credentials he holds expands his expertise in sports specific exercise, nutrition, fitness and lifestyle modification beyond the normal course of study of his colleagues.
